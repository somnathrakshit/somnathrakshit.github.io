Biography

I am a masters student (Data Science track) at the School of Information, The University of Texas at Austin and a graduate in Computer Science and Engineering from Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College. Previously, I was a research assistant at CeNT working on machine learning and bioinformatics. I also worked breiefly as a software engineer at Cyware Labs, Bangalore. My current interests lie in deep learning, natural language processing and knowledge graphs. I have worked on feature selection and ranking using machine learning on various biomedical datasets. I am currently working with Prof. Ying Ding.
