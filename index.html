About Me

I am a Masters student (Data Science track) at the School of Information, The University of Texas at Austin. I am a part of IDEAL and UT Computational and Imaging Lab here. Previously, I was a research assistant at CeNT working on machine learning and bioinformatics. I like working in natural language processing, predictive analytics and machine learning in general. I am currently working on my Master’s Thesis titled Sequence parameter selection for parameter mapping under Dr. Jon Tamir‘s supervision.
