Biography

I am a Masters student (Data Science track) at the School of Information and a Teaching Assistant for MIS 385N: User Generated Content Analytics at Department of Information, Risk and Operations Management, McCombs School of Business, The University of Texas at Asutin. Previously, I was a research assistant at CeNT working on machine learning and bioinformatics. I also worked breiefly as a software engineer at Cyware Labs, Bangalore. My current interests lie in deep learning, natural language processing and knowledge graphs. I am currently working with Prof. Ying Ding.
